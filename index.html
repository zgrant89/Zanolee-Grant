Zanolee Grant

Filmmaker, Archivist, Storyteller

About

Zanolee Grant is an independent filmmaker and researcher from Roatán, Honduras, currently based in South Florida. In her work, Grant utilizes oral history, family archives, and community-owned vernacular photography to explore themes such as migration, collective memory, and identity.
