About

Zanolee Grant is an independent filmmaker and researcher originally from Roatan, Honduras, based in South Florida. In her work, Grant utilizes qualitative methods and data such as oral history, family archives, and community-owned vernacular photography to explore themes such as migration, collective memory, and identity.
